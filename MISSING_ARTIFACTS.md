## Why these are not in v1.0

The repository was released to coincide with preprint submission. The author (single author, AIMR) prioritized verifying that committed artifacts are accurate, current, and consistent with the paper's calibrated claims rather than rushing all referenced supplementary materials into the v1.0 release. Several of the missing artifacts exist in earlier project state but require an audit pass before public release to ensure they reflect post-Block-39 citation corrections and post-Block-40 framing calibration.

## Plan for v1.1

Target: as audit and reconstruction work permits. The author will not commit to a fixed timeline because several of the missing artifacts may require substantive reconstruction rather than simple commit, and an over-promised timeline would compromise audit quality. Readers needing specific artifacts before they appear in v1.1 should contact the author directly (correspondence: matt@aimronline.org).

The v1.1 release will add the missing artifacts above, with each verified against the current paper.md framing. If any referenced artifact is determined not to exist or to require substantial reconstruction, the paper.md cross-references will be updated to reflect this honestly rather than restored as if the artifact were available. Honesty about what supplementary material backs the paper is more important than completeness of supplementary material.
